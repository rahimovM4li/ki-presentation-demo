ABOUT COMPANY

"Kontakt International“ LLC is a construction company that carries out the full range of work in the field of industrial and civil construction. We specialize in the construction, reconstruction, and maintenance of oil and gas extraction and transportation facilities, as well as in the implementation of residential and commercial construction projects.

We provide high-quality services across the entire construction chain of industrial, civil, and social infrastructure. We build comfortable, safe, and environmentally friendly spaces for living and working, unlocking the potential of territories, clients, and employees by working in the spirit of partnership and mutually beneficial cooperation.
